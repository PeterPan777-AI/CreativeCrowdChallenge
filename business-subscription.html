Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens to my competitions if I cancel my subscription?
Active competitions will continue until their set end date. After your subscription ends, you won't be able to create new competitions, but you'll still have access to view and download past submissions.
Can I customize the look and feel of my competitions?
Yes, all business plans include customization options. Premium plan subscribers get additional white-label features for complete branding control.
How do I manage intellectual property rights?
Our platform includes customizable terms for each competition, allowing you to specify IP rights transfer. We recommend clearly stating your requirements in the competition brief.
Is there a trial period available?
We offer a 14-day free trial for new business users. During this period, you can access all the features of the Professional plan.
